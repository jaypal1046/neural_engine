Question: What is Constitutional AI?

Ideal Answer: Constitutional AI (CAI) is Anthropic's self-improvement technique where the AI critiques its own answers against a fixed set of rules (the 'Constitution') — without needing human feedback for every example.

**The loop:**
1. AI generates an answer
2. AI checks the answer against each rule in the Constitution
3. AI flags violations and suggests improvements
4. The improved answer becomes training data

For Neural Studio, our Constitution has 20 rules covering helpfulness, honesty, structure, and domain-specific compression knowledge. Run it: `python server/rlhf_trainer.py --cai`
