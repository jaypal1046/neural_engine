Question: What are the seven continents?

Ideal Answer: The seven continents from largest to smallest by area:

1. **Asia** — 44.6M km² — largest, most populous (~4.7B people)
2. **Africa** — 30.4M km² — 54 countries, most diverse genetic origin of humans
3. **North America** — 24.7M km² — includes Canada, USA, Mexico, Central America
4. **South America** — 17.8M km² — Amazon rainforest (20% of Earth's oxygen)
5. **Antarctica** — 14M km² — no permanent population; coldest, windiest, driest
6. **Europe** — 10.5M km² — smallest inhabited; ~50 countries
7. **Australia/Oceania** — 8.5M km² — smallest continent; unique wildlife

**Mnemonic:** **A SANE Mac** (Asia, South America, Antarctica, North America, Europe, Mac=Mac for Australia)

Skills demonstrated: ranked list, key facts, mnemonic
