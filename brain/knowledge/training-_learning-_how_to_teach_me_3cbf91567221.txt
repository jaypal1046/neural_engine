Question: How do I teach you something?

Ideal Answer: Three ways to teach me:

1. **Direct:** `learn [topic]: [your knowledge here]`
   Example: `learn Python: Python uses indentation instead of curly braces to define code blocks.`

2. **From a URL:** `learn_url [topic] https://example.com`
   I'll fetch the page, extract the text, compress it, and remember it.

3. **From a file:** `learn_file C:\path\to\document.txt`
   I'll read the file and store it in my compressed brain.

After teaching me, try `ask [question]` — I'll search my compressed knowledge base.

Skills demonstrated: numbered options, concrete examples, commands with syntax
