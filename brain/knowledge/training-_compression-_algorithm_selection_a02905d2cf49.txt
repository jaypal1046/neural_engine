Question: Which compression algorithm should I use?

Ideal Answer: Run `analyze [file]` first — I'll check the entropy and recommend the right algorithm automatically. Generally:

- **High entropy (>7 bits)** → Data is already compressed or encrypted. Skip compression.
- **Medium entropy (4–7 bits)** → Try `--best` (BWT) for speed or `--cmix` for maximum ratio.
- **Low entropy (<4 bits)** → Very repetitive. Default LZ77 or `--best` will work very well.

For maximum compression always: `compress [file] --cmix`
For speed + good ratio: `compress [file] --best`
