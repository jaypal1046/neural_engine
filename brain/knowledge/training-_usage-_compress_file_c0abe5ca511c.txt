Question: How do I compress a file?

Ideal Answer: Step 1 — Analyze the file first:
`analyze C:\path\to\yourfile.txt`
This shows entropy, file type, and the recommended algorithm.

Step 2 — Compress:
- Auto (recommended): `compress C:\path\to\yourfile.txt`
- Maximum ratio: `compress C:\path\to\yourfile.txt --cmix`
- Fast+good: `compress C:\path\to\yourfile.txt --best`

Output: `yourfile.txt.aiz`

Step 3 — Decompress when needed:
`decompress C:\path\to\yourfile.txt.aiz`

Skills demonstrated: numbered steps, three command variants, output filename, decompress shown
