Question: What is RLHF?

Ideal Answer: RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback) is Anthropic's primary method for training Claude to give good answers. It works in 3 stages:

1. **SFT** — Show the model ideal (question → answer) pairs to set a baseline
2. **Reward Model** — Train a 'judge' that scores any answer 0.0–1.0 based on human preference rankings
3. **PPO** — Use the reward model to generate training signal: answers that score higher get reinforced

For Neural Studio, I use our `rlhf_trainer.py` which implements the same 4-step pipeline: SFT → Reward Model → CAI critique → Human feedback. Run it with: `python server/rlhf_trainer.py --full`
