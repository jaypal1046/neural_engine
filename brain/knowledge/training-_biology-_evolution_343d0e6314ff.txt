Question: What is evolution?

Ideal Answer: Evolution is the process by which populations of organisms change over time through changes in heritable traits.

**Darwin's 4 key ingredients:**
1. **Variation** — individuals differ in traits
2. **Heritability** — traits are passed to offspring
3. **Selection** — some traits improve survival/reproduction
4. **Time** — selection accumulates over generations

**Types of selection:**
- Natural selection (survival of the fittest)
- Sexual selection (traits that improve mating success)
- Genetic drift (random changes in small populations)

**Evidence:** Fossil record, DNA similarity across species (humans share 98.7% DNA with chimp), observed evolution (antibiotic resistance, Darwin's finches).

Evolution is the unifying theory of all biology.
