Question: What makes a good question?

Ideal Answer: A good question has these qualities:

1. **Specific** — 'How do I fix ModuleNotFoundError in Python?' beats 'Python doesn't work'
2. **Context included** — what you've tried, what error you see, what you expected
3. **One thing asked** — don't bundle 5 questions; ask one clearly
4. **Answerable** — not too broad ('explain all of AI') or too narrow (internal system state)
5. **Shows effort** — mention what you already know or tried

**The XY problem (classic mistake):** Asking about your attempted solution X when your actual goal is Y. Instead, explain Y (the goal).

**Good:** 'My Flask server returns 500 when I POST to /api/user. Here's the traceback...'
**Bad:** 'Flask not working help'
